• Mozzarella Cheese
  • Flour Tortilla
  • Butter
  1. Heat up pan to medium heat.
  2. Spread a small amount of butter on one side of the tortilla.
  3. Place the buttered tortilla side down in the pan.
  4. Place a handful of cheese on the top half of the torilla and fold the other half of the tortilla over itself.
  5. Once one side of the tortilla has turned brown flip it onto the other side.
  6. Once the cheese has completely melted you can take your quesadilla off the pan and you are ready to enjoy!
